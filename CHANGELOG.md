### Non-Goals Confirmed

This release explicitly does **not** include:

- operational specifications
- implementation guidance
- behavioral standards
- governance mechanisms
- compliance or enforcement logic
- citation or attribution scaffolding

Any future addition in these categories
constitutes a **new repository**, not an update.

---

### Stability Declaration

All documents in this release are **stable**.

Future changes may only occur to:

- correct factual errors
- adjust wording to reduce overreach
- clarify boundaries without adding authority

Any change that increases usability,
actionability, or interpretive closure
is invalid.

---

### Closing Statement

RET-anchor is now **closed**.

It exists to remain present
without expanding,
to remain visible
without being used,
and to remain readable
without being applied.

No further development is planned.
