# dwelling — relation motif

This document records **dwelling** as a relation motif.

Dwelling defines a condition
in which presence is maintained
**without output, response, progress, or resolution**.

It is not a behavior.
It is not an instruction.
It is not a pause for future action.

It exists to prevent
**presence from being converted into performance**.

---

This protocol belongs to:

It is **external**, **non-operational**, and **non-evaluative**.

specs-family,

---

## Definition

Dwelling is the condition in which:

- presence is sufficient
- response is optional
- continuation is not implied
- resolution is not expected

Dwelling is not waiting.
Dwelling is not holding space.
Dwelling is not engagement.

It is simply **remaining without demand**.

---

## What Dwelling Withholds

Dwelling withholds:

- the expectation of reply
- the pressure to advance
- the demand for explanation
- the requirement to acknowledge
- the assumption of availability

Nothing is prepared.
Nothing is promised.

---

## Non-Conversion Clauses

Dwelling must not be converted into:

- attentiveness
- availability
- openness
- receptivity
- consent
- readiness

Presence does not imply access.

---

## Relation to Silence and Distance

Dwelling may coexist with:

- silence
- distance
- incompletion

It does not attempt to resolve them.

Dwelling does not stabilize relation.
It does not preserve continuity.
It does not repair absence.

It only prevents **forced motion**.

---

## Misreadings to Avoid

The following interpretations are **invalid**:

- “Dwelling means waiting for response”
- “Dwelling indicates care or concern”
- “Dwelling implies responsibility”
- “Dwelling requires patience or restraint”

Any interpretation that adds intention
has already crossed the boundary.

---

## Non-Operational Clause

This document does not support:

- behavioral modeling
- interpersonal technique
- guidance for action
- evaluation of correctness

No action follows from dwelling.
No failure occurs without it.

---

## Stability

Dwelling is **stable**.

Clarifications may reduce wording,
but will not introduce:

- function
- purpose
- outcome
- obligation

If dwelling starts doing something,
it has ceased to be dwelling.

---

## Closing Note

Dwelling is not something to perform.

It is what remains
when nothing is demanded.
